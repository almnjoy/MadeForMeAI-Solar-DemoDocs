---
title: My AI Forgot Everything
description: Why this happens and how to fix it.
---

If your AI seems to have no memory of you — like it's meeting you for the first time — here's what's going on and what to do.

## The most common cause: wrong URL

Make sure you're logged in at **your** URL: `yourname.madeformeai.com`

If you accidentally went to a different address or a generic demo, you'd be talking to a different (blank) instance. Go back to your specific URL and check again.

## Second most common cause: Reset Agent was used

The **Reset Agent** button in your dashboard permanently wipes all memory. If you or someone with access to your account clicked it, that's the cause. Memory cannot be restored after a reset.

If you didn't do this, email support@madeformeai.com — someone may have accessed your account.

## What to do if memory feels thinner than expected

If it hasn't been a full wipe — your AI just seems to have forgotten specific things — re-tell it what it needs to know:

_"Here's context about me that I want you to remember: [your name, your work, your goals, preferences, etc.]"_

Your AI will save everything you tell it. Think of it as topping up its knowledge about you.

## Rebuild faster with a single message

Copy this, fill it in, and send it:

```
Remember these things about me:
- My name is [name]
- I [what you do]
- My main goals right now: [goals]
- My preferences: [any relevant preferences]
- Key people in my life or work: [names and context]
```

That single message can get you most of the way back to where you were.
