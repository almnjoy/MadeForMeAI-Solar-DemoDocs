---
title: Common Issues
description: Quick fixes for the most common problems people run into.
---

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="I can't log in">
    **Check your password first** — go to `yourname.madeformeai.com` and try logging in. If it fails, use the "Forgot password" option on the login page to request a reset link.

    **Still not working?** Email support@madeformeai.com from the address you signed up with. We'll verify your identity and get you back in.

    [Full login troubleshooting →](/troubleshooting/cant-log-in)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My AI forgot who I am">
    This usually means one of two things:

    1. **You accidentally used a different URL** — make sure you're at `yourname.madeformeai.com` and not a different URL
    2. **The Reset Agent button was used** — this permanently wipes memory. If you didn't do this yourself, email support@madeformeai.com

    If memory seems thinner than expected (not a full wipe), just remind your AI: _"Here's some context about me: [your details]."_ It will save everything you tell it.

    [More on memory →](/troubleshooting/my-ai-forgot-me)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My AI is slow or not responding">
    **Wait a moment** — if it's using a tool (searching the web, reading a file), it may just be working. Give it 15-30 seconds.

    **Try refreshing the page** and sending your message again.

    **Check your internet connection** — your AI is in the cloud, so a slow connection can affect response speed.

    If it's consistently slow or completely unresponsive, check the [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) to see if there's a known issue.

    [More troubleshooting →](/troubleshooting/slow-or-stuck)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I didn't get a welcome email after signing up">
    Check your **spam or junk folder** first. The email comes from `your-ai@madeformeai.com`.

    If it's not there, email billing-support@madeformeai.com with your name and the email you used to sign up. We'll get it sorted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="It said something about a 'tool call' and paused — is that normal?">
    Yes, completely normal. When your AI does something active — like searching the web or creating a file — it pauses briefly and shows you what it's doing. Give it a moment. It'll come back with the result.

    [What are tool calls? →](/understanding/tool-calls)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My AI can't see my computer files">
    Your AI runs in the cloud, not on your computer. It can't access your local files by default. To work with a file, upload it directly into the chat using the attachment button.

    For files stored in Google Drive, [connect Google Drive →](/connect/google-drive) and your AI can access them from there.

    [Full explanation →](/understanding/cloud-not-local)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="An app connection stopped working">
    App connections can occasionally drop (especially after token refreshes or password changes on the connected account).

    Tell your Setup Assistant: _"My [WhatsApp/Gmail/etc.] isn't working — can you reconnect it?"_ It will run a diagnostic and walk you through a fix.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I want to start completely fresh">
    Use the **Reset Agent** option in your account dashboard. This permanently wipes all memory, files, and conversation history. Your subscription stays active.

    **This cannot be undone.** Only use it if you genuinely want a blank slate.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
