---
title: AI Is Slow or Stuck
description: What to do when your AI isn't responding or feels sluggish.
---

## It's paused with a "tool call" message

This is normal. Your AI is doing something — searching the web, reading a file, creating something. It's not stuck, it's working. Give it 10–30 seconds.

[What are tool calls? →](/understanding/tool-calls)

## It's been more than a minute with no response

1. **Refresh the page** and resend your message. Long tasks occasionally time out — refreshing usually resolves it.
2. **Check your internet connection** — your AI is in the cloud, so a slow or dropped connection can cause this.
3. **Try a simpler message first** — if you sent a very complex request, break it into smaller steps.

## Responses feel slow in general

A few things affect speed:

- **Model choice** — The default "Smart Assistant (DeepSeek)" model is fast for most tasks. If you've switched to a different model, try switching back.
- **Internet speed** — AI responses stream over your connection. A slow connection means slow delivery.
- **Time of day** — Peak usage times can occasionally cause higher response times across AI services globally.

## It stopped mid-response

If the response cut off partway through, say: _"Continue"_ or _"Keep going"_ and it will pick up where it left off.

## Complete unresponsiveness (no reaction at all)

If the chat is completely frozen — typing does nothing, no response:

1. Hard refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
2. Clear your browser cache if the hard refresh doesn't help
3. Try a different browser

If none of that works, check the [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) to see if there's a known outage or maintenance window.
