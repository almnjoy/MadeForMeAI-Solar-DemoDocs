title: Can't Log In
description: Step-by-step fix for login problems.
---

Can't get into your AI? Here's how to get back in.

## Step 1: Make sure you're at the right URL

Your AI lives at `yourname.madeformeai.com` — the name you chose when you signed up. Double-check you're going to the right address.

If you're not sure what your name is, go to [madeformeai.com/dashboard](https://madeformeai.com/dashboard) — it shows your AI's URL once you're logged in.

## Step 2: Reset your password

Go to [madeformeai.com/dashboard](https://madeformeai.com/dashboard) and click **Forgot password** (or use the Forgot password link on your AI's login page). Enter your email address and you'll receive a reset link.

<Warning>Check your spam folder. Emails come from `your-ai@madeformeai.com`. Add it to your contacts to prevent future filtering.</Warning>

## Step 3: Try a different browser or device

Sometimes a cached session causes login issues. Open an incognito/private window, or try a different browser entirely. If that works, clear your browser cookies for `madeformeai.com`.

## Step 4: Still not working?

Reach out in [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) or email **support@madeformeai.com** from the address you used to sign up. Include:

- The URL you're trying to access
- What happens when you try to log in (error message, blank screen, redirect loop, etc.)

---

## Common causes

| Symptom | Likely cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Password rejected | Typed wrong, or account uses a different email | Try password reset |
| Blank screen or spinning | Browser cache issue | Try incognito or clear cookies |
| "No server available" | Your AI workspace is still starting up | Wait 2–3 minutes and refresh |
| Redirect loop | Session conflict | Clear cookies and try again |
| Subscription lapsed | Payment failed | Check billing at the dashboard or email billing-support@madeformeai.com |
