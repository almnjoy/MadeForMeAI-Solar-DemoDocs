description: Step-by-step fix for login problems.
---

Can't get into your AI? Here's how to get back in.

## Step 1: Make sure you're at the right URL

Your AI lives at `yourname.madeformeai.com` — the name you chose when you signed up. Double-check you're going to the right address.

If you're not sure what your name is, try the dashboard: [madeformeai.com/dashboard](https://madeformeai.com/dashboard)

## Step 2: Try resetting your password

On the login page at your AI's URL, look for a "Forgot password" or "Send login link" option. Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link.

<Warning>Check your spam folder. Emails come from `your-ai@madeformeai.com`. Add it to your contacts to prevent future filtering.</Warning>

## Step 3: Still not working?

Email **support@madeformeai.com** from the address you used to sign up. Include:

- The URL you're trying to access
- The email address you signed up with
- What happens when you try to log in (error message, blank screen, etc.)

We'll verify your identity and get you back in.

## Common causes

- **Expired session** — just log in again
- **Wrong URL** — make sure it's `yourname.madeformeai.com`, not a generic page
- **Wrong email** — if you have multiple email addresses, try the others
- **Payment issue** — if your subscription lapsed, access is paused until billing is resolved. Email billing-support@madeformeai.com.
