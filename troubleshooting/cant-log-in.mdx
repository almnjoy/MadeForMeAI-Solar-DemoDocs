If you can't get into your MadeForMeAI account, you're almost certainly dealing with one of a handful of easy-to-fix issues — a partial token paste, a wrong URL, or a browser quirk. Work through the steps below in order and you'll be back in your AI in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your token">
    Your login token is a long string sent in your welcome email. It's easy to accidentally copy only part of it.

    A valid token looks like this:

    ```
    f655e5d4357630e1dd6e195d9b6188ce1deeac18e09a647fc14a8a1737a99a56
    ```

    Copy it directly from your email — do not retype it. Make sure you capture the full string from start to finish with no extra spaces.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm you're on the right URL">
    Your MadeForMeAI instance lives at a personal subdomain — `yourname.madeformeai.com` — the name you chose when you signed up. Going to just `madeformeai.com` will not log you in.

    Check your welcome email for your exact URL if you're unsure what your subdomain is.

    <Tip>
      Bookmark your personal URL now so you always open the right page.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Try a different browser or incognito mode">
    If the login page isn't loading or the token isn't being accepted, open an incognito or private browsing window and try again. If that works, clear your main browser's cache.

    If you're still stuck, try Chrome or Firefox as an alternative browser.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact support if the token still won't work">
    If your token is definitely correct and you're on the right URL but still can't get in, reach out for help:

    - **Discord:** [discord.gg/madeformeai](https://discord.gg/madeformeai)
    - **Email:** support@madeformeai.com

    Include your username and subdomain in your message so the team can look up your account quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lost your token

If you've lost your token entirely and can't access your account, email **support@madeformeai.com** from the same email address you used to sign up. The team will verify your identity and issue you a new token.

<Warning>
  Always send your lost-token request from your signup email address. This is how the team confirms your identity — requests from a different address can't be processed.
</Warning>

## Save your token for next time

Once you're back in, take a moment to store your token somewhere safe.

<Tip>
  Save your token in a password manager (such as 1Password, Bitwarden, or your browser's built-in manager) alongside your personal URL. This way you'll never be locked out again.
</Tip>
