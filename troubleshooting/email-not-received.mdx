Your welcome email contains your login token and personal URL — everything you need to access your AI for the first time. If it hasn't shown up in your inbox, don't worry. It's almost always sitting in spam, or it just needs another minute to arrive.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Wait up to 2 minutes">
    Your MadeForMeAI instance takes up to 2 minutes to provision after you complete checkout. The welcome email sends automatically once your AI is ready. If you just finished signing up, give it a couple of minutes before you start troubleshooting.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your spam and junk folders">
    This is the most common reason welcome emails go missing. Open your spam or junk folder and look for an email from `your-ai@madeformeai.com`.

    <Tip>
      Search across all folders — including Promotions, Updates, and Spam — for `madeformeai` or `your-ai@madeformeai.com` to find it quickly.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add us to your contacts">
    To make sure future emails from MadeForMeAI land in your inbox, add `your-ai@madeformeai.com` to your contacts now. This tells your email provider to trust messages from this address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Still nothing after 5 minutes?

If it's been more than 5 minutes and you can't find the email anywhere, get in touch with the support team directly.

- **Discord:** [discord.gg/madeformeai](https://discord.gg/madeformeai)
- **Email:** support@madeformeai.com

When you reach out, include:

- The email address you used to sign up
- Roughly when you completed checkout

The team will look up your account and send your login details manually.

<Warning>
  Make sure you contact support from the same email address you used at checkout. This is how the team locates your account.
</Warning>
