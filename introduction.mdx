MadeForMeAI gives you a personal AI assistant that belongs entirely to you — not a shared platform where millions of other people are doing the same thing. It lives at its own private URL, remembers everything you tell it across every conversation, and connects to the tools you already use. Think of it less like a chatbot and more like a dedicated assistant who actually knows you.

## What makes it different

Most AI tools forget you the moment you close the tab. MadeForMeAI doesn't. Here's what sets it apart:

**It remembers you.** Tell it something on Monday and it still knows on Friday. You never have to copy-paste the same background about yourself into every new session.

**It's only yours.** Your conversations, your files, your data — none of it is shared, none of it trains any AI model, and none of it is read by anyone else.

**It works with your stuff.** Connect Gmail, Google Calendar, or your files so your AI fits into how you already work, rather than asking you to change your workflow for it.

**It actually does things.** Drafts emails. Plans your week. Organizes files. Searches the web. It goes well beyond answering questions.

## Your own URL

When you sign up, you choose a name that becomes your private address — for example, `mark.madeformeai.com`. That URL is yours alone. Nobody else uses it, and nobody else can access it without your login token.
