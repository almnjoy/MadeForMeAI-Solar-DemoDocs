Getting your AI assistant up and running takes less than two minutes. You'll choose a name for your AI's private URL, pick a plan, and log in with the token that arrives in your welcome email. No app to install, no configuration required — just sign up and start talking.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your AI's name">
    Go to [madeformeai.com/signup](https://madeformeai.com/signup) and type the name you want for your AI's URL.

    For example, type `mark` and your AI will live at `mark.madeformeai.com`.

    <Note>
      Use letters and numbers only, between 3 and 20 characters. You cannot change this name after signup, so pick something you're happy with long-term.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a plan and check out">
    Select BYOK, Standard, or Power — then click **Get started**. You'll go through a quick checkout. There's no long-term commitment; you can cancel anytime.

    <Tip>
      Not sure which plan to choose? Standard is the right fit for most people. If you already have an OpenAI or Anthropic API key, BYOK saves you money.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your email">
    Within a minute or two, you'll receive an email from `your-ai@madeformeai.com` containing your login token and your AI's URL.

    <Warning>
      Check your spam folder if you don't see the email. Add `your-ai@madeformeai.com` to your contacts to make sure future emails land in your inbox.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in with your token">
    Go to your AI's URL (for example, `mark.madeformeai.com`). When prompted, paste in the token from your welcome email and press enter.

    <Warning>
      Save that token somewhere safe — a password manager, a secure note, or anywhere you won't lose it. It's the only way to log in, and we can't recover it for you.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start talking">
    That's it. Just say what you need. No setup steps, no tutorial, no learning curve. Your AI is ready to go the moment you're in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
