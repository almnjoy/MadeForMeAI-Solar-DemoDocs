title: Get Your AI in 2 Minutes
description: From sign-up to your first conversation — no tech skills needed.
---

This literally takes 2 minutes. Here's the whole thing:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a name for your AI">
    Go to [madeformeai.com/early-access](https://madeformeai.com/early-access) and type the name you want for your AI's link.

    Type `sarah` and your AI lives at `sarah.madeformeai.com`. Type `myai` and it's at `myai.madeformeai.com`. Whatever you want — it's your address.

    <Note>Use letters, numbers, or hyphens. Between 3 and 32 characters. You can't change this after you sign up, so pick something you'll remember.</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a plan and check out">
    Pick **Standard** if you're not sure — it's right for most people. Everything is included and you're ready to go immediately.

    Click **Get started**, fill in your card details, and you're done. No contracts. Cancel anytime.

    <Note>Your first 30 days are included after activation. Try it, see how it fits.</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait about 2 minutes — your AI is being built">
    After checkout you'll land on a setup page. Your personal AI assistant is being created just for you — this takes about 2 minutes.

    The page will automatically take you to the next step when it's ready. Just leave it open.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set a password and log in">
    When setup finishes, you'll be sent to a secure page to set your account password. Do that, then go to your AI's URL — something like `yourname.madeformeai.com`.

    Log in with your email and the password you just set. That's it.

    <Warning>If you don't receive the setup email, check your spam folder. Add `your-ai@madeformeai.com` to your contacts now so future emails get through.</Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Say hello and start talking">
    Your AI is ready. There's no tutorial, no setup wizard, nothing to configure. Just say what's on your mind.

    Not sure what to say? Try one of these:

    - _"What can you help me with?"_
    - _"I run a small cleaning business. Help me write a follow-up message to send clients after a job."_
    - _"I have a lot going on this week. Help me get organized."_
    - _"Remember that my name is [your name] and I work in [your field]."_

    The more you talk to it, the better it gets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add it to your phone

Your AI works great on your phone right from the browser — no app store needed. Add it to your home screen so it opens like a real app.

On iPhone: open Safari → tap the **Share** button → tap **Add to Home Screen**.

On Android: open Chrome → tap the three-dot menu → tap **Add to Home Screen**.
