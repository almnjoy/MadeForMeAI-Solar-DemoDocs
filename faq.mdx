<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to know anything about AI to use this?">
    Nope. Zero. If you can text someone or send an email, you can use this. Just type what you need like you're talking to a person. There's nothing technical to set up or figure out.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this the same as ChatGPT?">
    No — and that difference is exactly the point. ChatGPT has hundreds of millions of users, forgets everything the second you close the tab, and is built for everyone in general. MadeForMeAI is yours specifically. It remembers you, knows your context, and nobody else shares it with you. Think of it less like a search engine and more like a personal assistant who actually knows who you are.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my stuff private?">
    Yes, completely. Your conversations, your files, and anything you share with your AI stay locked to you. Nothing you say gets shared with anyone, used to train AI models, or read by us. It's your private space.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does it learn about me?">
    By talking with you. The more you use it, the more it understands how you think, what you care about, and how you like things done. You can also just tell it things directly — "my name is Sarah, I run a bakery, and I'm terrible at writing emails" — and it will remember all of that going forward.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="It paused and said something about a 'tool' — is that normal?">
    Yes, completely normal. When your AI is doing something active — like searching the web, creating a file, or looking something up — it may briefly pause and show you what it's doing. That's it actually working, not broken. Give it a few seconds and it'll come back with the result. [Learn more about tool calls →](/understanding/tool-calls)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why can't my AI see files on my computer?">
    Your AI runs in the cloud, not on your computer. It can't browse your desktop, your Downloads folder, or your local files — unless you upload them into the chat directly. This trips a lot of people up at first. [Here's a simple explanation →](/understanding/cloud-not-local)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can it actually do?">
    Draft and send emails, write documents, search the web and summarize what it finds, plan your week or your business launch, help you think through a decision, answer questions, analyze files you upload, keep notes, set reminders, and a lot more. Once you connect your apps (like WhatsApp or Google Drive), it can do even more. [See real examples →](/understanding/what-it-can-do)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I talk to it on my phone?">
    Yes. Your AI works great in your phone's browser — no app needed. Open Safari or Chrome, go to your AI's URL, and it works. You can also add it to your home screen so it opens like a real app. [Here's how →](/getting-started/add-to-phone)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is BYOK?">
    BYOK stands for Bring Your Own Key. If you already pay for OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter separately, you can use your own API key with MadeForMeAI on the $15/month BYOK plan. You pay your AI provider directly for usage, and we just run your private setup. [More on BYOK →](/byok/setup)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no forms to fill out. Email billing-support@madeformeai.com and it's done. Your data is held for 30 days after cancellation in case you change your mind, then permanently deleted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
    Everything — conversations, files, memory — is kept for 30 days. If you come back within 30 days we can restore it all. After 30 days it's gone permanently.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own domain?">
    Not yet. Custom domain support (like `ai.yourcompany.com`) is on the roadmap. Right now your AI lives at `yourname.madeformeai.com`.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get help?">
    The fastest way is the [Discord community](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) — there are real people there including Dustin. You can also email support@madeformeai.com.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
