title: Logging In
description: How to access your AI — it's just a username and password.
---

MadeForMeAI uses standard account login — your email and the password you set when you first activated your account. No tokens, no API keys, nothing unusual.

## How to log in

**Option 1 — From your dashboard:**

Go to [madeformeai.com/dashboard](https://madeformeai.com/dashboard), sign in with your email and password, then click **Launch AI**. You'll land directly in your AI's chat window.

**Option 2 — Direct URL:**

Go straight to your AI's address — something like `yourname.madeformeai.com`. You'll see a login screen. Enter your email and password and you're in.

Both options go to the same place. Most people bookmark the direct URL.

---

## Staying logged in

Once you log in on a device, your browser remembers the session. You won't need to sign in again unless you clear your cookies or use a different browser or device.

---

## Forgot your password?

On the login screen, click **Forgot password** and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a couple of minutes.

<Warning>Check your spam folder. Email comes from `your-ai@madeformeai.com`. Add it to your contacts so it doesn't get filtered.</Warning>

---

## Logging in on a new device

Just go to your AI's URL or the dashboard on the new device and sign in with your email and password. Your AI, memory, and files are all in the cloud — nothing lives on the device itself.
