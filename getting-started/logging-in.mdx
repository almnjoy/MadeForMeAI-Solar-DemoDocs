MadeForMeAI uses a **login token** instead of a traditional password. Your token was sent to you in your welcome email the moment your account was created. Once you log in on a device, your browser remembers your session — so you only need to paste the token again when you're on a new device or browser.

## How to log in

1. Go to your AI's URL (for example, `mark.madeformeai.com`)
2. You'll see a prompt asking for your login token
3. Copy the token from your welcome email and paste it in
4. Press enter — you're in

## Finding your token

Your token is in the welcome email from `your-ai@madeformeai.com`, with the subject line **"Your AI is ready"**. It looks like a long string of random letters and numbers:

```text
f655e5d4357630e1dd6e195d9b6188ce1deeac18e09a647fc14a8a1737a99a56
```

<Warning>
  Save your token somewhere safe — a password manager is ideal. We cannot recover your token for you if it's lost. Treat it like a password.
</Warning>

## Staying logged in

Once you log in on a device, your browser remembers your session. You won't need to paste your token every single time — only when you're on a new browser, a new device, or if you've cleared your browser data.

## Logging in on a new device

Just paste your token again on the new device. Each device you log in from will be remembered separately going forward.

## Lost your token?

If you've lost your token and can't get back in, reach out for help:

- Join the [Discord community](https://discord.gg/madeformeai) and post in the support channel
- Email the team at support@madeformeai.com

The team will verify your account and get you back in.
