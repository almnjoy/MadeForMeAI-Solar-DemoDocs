The moment you log in, your AI is ready to talk. There's no setup wizard, no prompts to configure, and nothing to learn before you can start. Just type what's on your mind — the same way you'd send a message to someone you trust to help you.

## Just talk to it

You don't need special commands or precise phrasing. Write naturally, like you're texting someone. A few things to try right away:

- *"What can you help me with?"*
- *"Here's what I'm working on this week — help me prioritize."*
- *"Draft an email to my team about our meeting tomorrow."*
- *"Summarize this document for me"* — then paste in the text

Your AI will respond directly and get to work. If it needs more detail, it'll ask.

## Tell it about yourself

The more your AI knows about you, the more useful it becomes. Start by giving it a quick picture of who you are and what you do. For example:

> *"I'm a freelance designer. I work with small business clients, usually on branding and websites. I'm pretty busy and need help staying organized."*

You only have to say this once. Your AI will remember it and use that context in every future conversation, without you having to repeat yourself.

## It remembers everything you tell it

Unlike most AI tools, your MadeForMeAI assistant keeps memory across conversations. Anything you share stays in its memory until you ask it to forget.

This means:

- It gets more useful and more personalized the longer you use it
- You don't repeat the same background information every session
- It can reference things you told it weeks ago without you prompting it

<Tip>
  The more context you give your AI early on — your work, your preferences, your goals — the faster it becomes genuinely helpful.
</Tip>
