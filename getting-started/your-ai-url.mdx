When you signed up, you picked a name — and that became your AI's address on the internet.

Something like: **yourname.madeformeai.com**

This is your private link to your assistant. Bookmark it. Add it to your phone. That's all you need to access your AI from anywhere.

## It's completely private

Nobody else can access your AI's URL. If someone else goes to `yourname.madeformeai.com`, they'll hit a login screen — and they don't have your password.

Your conversations, your files, your memory — none of it is accessible to anyone else. It's yours.

## You can use it from anywhere

Your AI works on:

- Your laptop or desktop (any browser)
- Your phone (Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android)
- A tablet
- Even a shared computer (just log out when you're done)

You don't need to install anything. Just go to your URL and log in.

## Add it to your phone home screen

You can make your AI feel like a real app by adding it to your phone's home screen. When you tap it, it opens full-screen with no browser bars — just your assistant.

[How to add it to your phone →](/getting-started/add-to-phone)

## What if you forget your URL?

Your URL is always `yourname.madeformeai.com` — whatever name you chose when you signed up. You can also log in through your account dashboard at [madeformeai.com/dashboard](https://madeformeai.com/dashboard).

If you truly can't remember your name, email support@madeformeai.com from the address you signed up with.
