When you sign up for MadeForMeAI, you choose a name that becomes the permanent address for your AI assistant. That address is private by default — only you can get in — and it runs around the clock so your AI is always there when you need it.

## What your URL looks like

If you chose the name `mark` during signup, your AI lives at:

```
mark.madeformeai.com
```

Every MadeForMeAI account has its own unique subdomain like this. No two users share the same URL.

## What this means for you

**Only you can access it.** Your URL is private by default. Anyone who visits it without your login token will be turned away. Your conversations and data stay yours.

**It's always on.** Your AI runs 24/7. You can close your browser, come back tomorrow, come back next month — it picks up right where you left off.

**Bookmark it.** Add `yourname.madeformeai.com` to your browser bookmarks so you can get back to your AI in one click.

## Can I change my URL?

Not currently. The name you chose during signup is permanent. Pick it with care — something short and easy to remember works best.

<Note>
  If you genuinely need your URL changed, reach out in the [Discord community](https://discord.gg/madeformeai) and the team will sort it out manually.
</Note>

## Can I use a custom domain?

Custom domain support — for example, pointing `ai.yoursite.com` to your assistant — is on the roadmap but not available yet.
