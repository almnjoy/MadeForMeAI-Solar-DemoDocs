---
title: Add Your AI to Your Phone
description: Make your AI feel like a native app — no app store required.
---

Your AI works in any mobile browser, but the best experience is adding it to your home screen. When you do, it opens full-screen — no browser bars, no URL visible — just your assistant.

It takes about 30 seconds.

## iPhone (Safari)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your AI in Safari">
    Go to `yourname.madeformeai.com` in Safari. (This has to be Safari — it won't work from Chrome on iPhone for this specific step.)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap the Share button">
    At the bottom of the screen, tap the **Share** icon — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Add to Home Screen">
    Scroll down in the share menu until you see **Add to Home Screen**. Tap it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give it a name and tap Add">
    You can change the name if you want (or leave it). Tap **Add** in the top right corner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your AI now appears on your home screen like a real app. Tap it and it opens full-screen.

---

## Android (Chrome)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your AI in Chrome">
    Go to `yourname.madeformeai.com` in Chrome.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Look for the install banner">
    Chrome will usually show a banner at the bottom that says **Add to Home Screen** or **Install app**. Tap it.

    If you don't see the banner, tap the **three dots** in the top-right corner of Chrome.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Add to Home Screen">
    In the menu, tap **Add to Home Screen**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Add">
    Confirm by tapping **Add** on the popup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Why bother?

Once it's on your home screen:

- One tap to open, no typing a URL
- Full screen with no browser chrome — feels like a real app
- Works faster because your browser keeps the page cached
- You can use it the same way you'd use any other app throughout your day

Most people who use their AI daily add it to their home screen within the first week.
