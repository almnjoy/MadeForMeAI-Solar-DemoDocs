Your AI is useful on day one — but it gets significantly more powerful when it's connected to the tools you already use.

## What you can connect

### Messaging — talk to your AI from your phone

These connectors let you chat with your AI through apps already on your phone. No browser tab needed.

| App | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| **WhatsApp** | Text your AI like a contact — ask questions, get reminders, run tasks |
| **Telegram** | Same as WhatsApp — use whichever app you prefer |
| **Discord** | Chat with your AI in a Discord server or DM |

### Google & Web

| Integration | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| **Google Drive** | Read, create, and organize files in your Drive via Google API |
| **Web Search** | Your AI searches the web automatically when it needs current information — no setup needed, it's on by default |

---

## How does setup work?

You don't do any of it yourself. That's what the **Setup Assistant** is for.

In your AI's chat window, just say:

- _"Set up WhatsApp for me"_
- _"Connect Telegram"_
- _"I want to use Discord"_

The Setup Assistant walks you through each step with plain-English instructions. No config files, no commands.

[Learn more about the Setup Assistant →](/features/setup-assistant)

---

## Is it private?

When you connect an app, only your AI can access it. Not us, not other users — no one. You can disconnect anything at any time by telling the Setup Assistant: _"Disconnect WhatsApp"_ or _"Remove Telegram."_
