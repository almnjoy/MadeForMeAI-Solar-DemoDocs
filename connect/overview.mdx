Your AI is useful on day one — but it gets significantly more powerful when you connect it to the apps you already use every day.

## Why connect apps?

Right now, your AI can only know things you've told it directly or that it found on the web. When you connect apps, it can:

- Read and respond to your actual emails
- Access files you already have saved
- Send messages on your behalf through apps you use
- Keep track of events in your calendar

You don't have to connect anything. But if you want your AI to feel like a real assistant rather than just a chat window, connecting at least one app is the next step.

## What you can connect

| App | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| **WhatsApp** | Talk to your AI by texting it — like a real contact on your phone |
| **Telegram** | Same as WhatsApp — another option if you use Telegram |
| **Gmail** | Read, summarize, draft, and send emails |
| **Google Drive** | Access, read, create, and organize files in your Drive |

More connectors are being added over time. Join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) to see what's coming or request a specific app.

## How does setup work?

You don't do any of it yourself. That's what the **Setup Assistant** is for.

In your AI's chat window, just say something like:

- _"Set up WhatsApp for me"_
- _"Connect my Gmail"_
- _"I want to use Telegram"_

The Setup Assistant will walk you through each step, one at a time, with plain-English instructions. You won't need to touch any configuration files or run any commands.

[Learn more about the Setup Assistant →](/features/setup-assistant)

## How private is this?

When you connect an app, only your AI can access it. Nobody else — not us, not other users, no one. Your emails, files, and messages stay private to you.

You can also disconnect any app at any time by telling your Setup Assistant: _"Disconnect my Gmail"_ or _"Remove WhatsApp."_
