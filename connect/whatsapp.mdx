title: Connect WhatsApp
description: Talk to your AI by texting it — like a real contact on your phone.
---

Once WhatsApp is connected, you can text your AI the same way you'd text a friend. It responds in the same chat. No need to open a browser.

This is one of the most popular features — especially if you're on your phone all day.

## Before you start

You need:
- WhatsApp installed on your phone and signed in
- Access to your AI's chat window (open `yourname.madeformeai.com` in a browser)

## How to set it up

You don't configure anything yourself. Just talk to your Setup Assistant.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your AI and ask for setup">
    Go to `yourname.madeformeai.com` and type:

    _"Set up WhatsApp for me"_

    Your Setup Assistant will take it from there.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Your AI will send you a QR code">
    In a moment, your AI will display a QR code image directly in the chat window.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scan the QR code from WhatsApp">
    On your phone, open WhatsApp and go to:

    **Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device**

    Point your camera at the QR code on your screen. WhatsApp will scan it and connect automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="You're connected">
    That's it. Your AI is now a contact in your WhatsApp. Send it a message to test it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What can you do with it?

Everything you can do in the chat window — over WhatsApp text:

- Ask questions
- Get summaries, plans, drafts
- Tell it to do things ("draft an email to my client")
- Have it search the web for you
- Share a photo and ask it to describe or analyze it

## What if the QR code expires?

QR codes for WhatsApp expire after a few minutes. If yours expired before you could scan it, just tell your AI: _"Send me a new WhatsApp QR code"_ and it will generate a fresh one.

## Disconnect WhatsApp

To unlink WhatsApp, tell your Setup Assistant: _"Disconnect WhatsApp."_

You can also unlink it from your phone: **WhatsApp → Settings → Linked Devices** → tap your AI's device → **Log Out**.
