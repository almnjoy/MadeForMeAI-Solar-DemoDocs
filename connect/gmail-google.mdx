title: Connect Gmail
description: Let your AI read, draft, and send emails on your behalf.
---

Once Gmail is connected, your AI becomes a real email assistant. It can check your inbox, summarize what's waiting for you, draft replies, and send emails — all from a conversation.

## Before you start

You need a Google account with Gmail. That's it.

## How to set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask your Setup Assistant">
    Open your AI's chat and type:

    _"Connect my Gmail"_

    Your Setup Assistant will guide you through the full process.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in with Google">
    Your Setup Assistant will direct you to a Google sign-in page. Sign in with the Gmail account you want to connect, and grant the requested permissions.

    These permissions let your AI read and send email on your behalf. You can revoke them at any time through your Google account settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm it's working">
    Your Setup Assistant will run a quick check to confirm the connection is active.

    Then try it: _"Check my inbox and tell me what needs attention."_
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can do with connected Gmail

**Read and summarize:**
- _"Check my inbox and tell me what's important."_
- _"Summarize the email from [name] about [topic]."_
- _"Do I have any unread emails from my bank?"_

**Draft and reply:**
- _"Draft a reply to Kim's email — tell her I'll have the report ready by Thursday."_
- _"Write a professional follow-up to anyone I haven't heard back from in two weeks."_

**Send:**
- _"Send that draft to Kim."_ (Your AI will confirm before sending anything.)

## Your AI will always confirm before sending

Your AI won't fire off emails without checking with you first. It will show you the draft and ask if you want to send it. You say yes, it sends. Simple.

## Google Calendar too

When you connect Gmail, Google Calendar often comes along with it. Ask your Setup Assistant: _"Can you also access my Google Calendar?"_ to enable events and scheduling.

## Privacy

Only your AI can access your Gmail. We cannot read your emails. Nothing is shared or stored outside your private setup. You can disconnect Gmail at any time by telling your Setup Assistant: _"Disconnect Gmail."_
