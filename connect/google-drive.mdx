---
title: Connect Google Drive
description: Let your AI access, read, create, and organize files in your Google Drive.
---

Once Google Drive is connected, your AI can work with the files you already have stored there — instead of you having to upload everything one by one.

## Before you start

You need a Google account with Drive access. If you use Gmail, you already have Google Drive.

## How to set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask your Setup Assistant">
    Open your AI's chat and type:

    _"Connect my Google Drive"_

    Your Setup Assistant will walk you through it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in with Google">
    You'll be directed to a Google sign-in page. Sign in and grant the requested permissions. These let your AI read and create files in your Drive.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test it">
    Once connected, try: _"What files do I have in my Google Drive?"_ or _"Find the document about [topic] in my Drive."_
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can do

**Find and read files:**
- _"Find the proposal I wrote last month."_
- _"Open the spreadsheet called Budget 2026 and summarize it."_
- _"Do I have anything in my Drive related to [topic]?"_

**Create new files:**
- _"Create a Google Doc with the business plan we just built."_
- _"Save our conversation summary to a new file in my Drive."_

**Organize:**
- _"Move the files in my Downloads folder to [a specific Drive folder]."_
- _"Create a new folder called Client Projects."_

## Privacy

Only your AI accesses your Drive. Nothing is shared outside your private setup. Revoke access at any time through Google Account settings or by telling your Setup Assistant: _"Disconnect Google Drive."_
