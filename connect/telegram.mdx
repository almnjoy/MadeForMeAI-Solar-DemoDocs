Telegram is one of the easiest apps to connect. Once it's set up, your AI lives inside Telegram as a private bot — you message it, it messages back.

## Before you start

You need:
- Telegram installed on your phone or computer, and signed in
- Access to your AI's chat window (open `yourname.madeformeai.com` in a browser)

## How to set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask your Setup Assistant">
    Open your AI's chat and type:

    _"Set up Telegram for me"_

    The Setup Assistant will guide you through every step.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a bot in Telegram">
    Your Setup Assistant will tell you to open Telegram and start a chat with **@BotFather** — this is Telegram's official bot that creates new bots. It's just a chat inside Telegram, nothing complicated.

    When @BotFather asks, follow the prompts to create a new bot and give it a name. You'll get a token — a long string of numbers and letters.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share the token with your Setup Assistant">
    Paste that token back into the chat with your Setup Assistant. It will handle the rest of the configuration.

    <Warning>Treat your bot token like a password. Don't share it publicly or post it anywhere. If it ever gets leaked, your Setup Assistant can generate a new one.</Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start the bot in Telegram">
    Your Setup Assistant will tell you to find your new bot in Telegram (search for the name you gave it) and send it a message to activate it.

    That's it — you're connected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using your AI on Telegram

Once connected, find your bot in Telegram and just start talking:

- _"What's on my to-do list today?"_
- _"Draft a response to the email from my landlord"_
- _"Search the web for cheap flights to Miami next month"_

Everything works through regular Telegram messages. You can use it on your phone, tablet, or desktop — wherever Telegram is installed.

## Disconnect Telegram

Tell your Setup Assistant: _"Disconnect Telegram"_ and it will remove the connection.
