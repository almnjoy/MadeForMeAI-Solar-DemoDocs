# Install Process

NorthernPWR handles the entire process — from site assessment to activation. Here's what the journey looks like.

## Step 1 — Free Consultation
A solar advisor reviews your energy usage, roof layout, and location. No pressure. You get real numbers and a clear picture of what solar would look like for your home. Bring 12 months of utility bills — that's what drives system sizing.

## Step 2 — Custom System Design
Using satellite imagery, NPWR assesses your roof dimensions, orientation, and best panel placement. The system is sized to match your actual consumption patterns.

## Step 3 — On-Site Survey
Physical inspection of your roof and main electrical panel to verify the design before permitting.

## Step 4 — Permitting
NPWR handles all permits with local authorities. Timeline varies by municipality.

## Step 5 — Installation
Professional crew installs panels, racking, inverters, and wiring. Full safety protocols — PPE, OHS compliance, site inspections.

## Step 6 — Monitoring Setup
You're set up with a monitoring portal to track energy production and receive alerts. Checking it weekly is recommended.

## Step 7 — Activation
System is connected to the grid and live.

## What to Expect

- Most of the process is handled by NPWR — minimal action required from you after signing
- You're kept informed at every stage
- NPWR guarantees the system produces at least 85% of estimated annual energy in your contract

## Warranties

- **Panels:** 25-year performance warranty (manufacturer)
- **Workmanship:** NPWR installation warranty included
- **Other components:** Individual manufacturer warranties
