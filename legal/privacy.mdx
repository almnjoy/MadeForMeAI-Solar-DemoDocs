**Last updated: May 2026**

MadeForMeAI is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. Here's exactly what that means.

## What we collect

**Account information** — your email address and the username you chose when you signed up. This is used to manage your account and contact you about your subscription.

**Billing information** — processed by Stripe. We do not store your card number. Stripe handles all payment processing securely.

**Usage metadata** — basic technical logs (server uptime, error rates, response times). This does not include the content of your conversations.

## What we do NOT collect

- The content of your conversations with your AI
- Files you upload into your AI
- Data from connected apps (Gmail, Google Drive, etc.)
- Anything that would allow us to identify what you said or asked

Your AI's memory and conversations are stored in your private instance. We do not have access to them, we do not read them, and we do not use them to train AI models.

## Your data stays in your instance

Your AI runs in a private container dedicated to you. Your conversations, memory, and files are stored within that container. They are not shared with other users, not processed by us, and not used for any purpose outside of running your personal AI.

## Third-party AI providers

Your AI uses language models from third-party providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, or OpenRouter depending on your plan). When you send a message, it is processed by that provider's API. Each provider has their own privacy policy governing how they handle API inputs. We recommend reviewing their policies if this is important to you.

## Data deletion

If you cancel your account, your data is held for 30 days and then permanently deleted — including all conversations, memory, and files. If you want it deleted sooner, email support@madeformeai.com.
