## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up and pick a name">
    Go to [madeformeai.com/signup](https://madeformeai.com/signup), choose a name for your AI's URL, and select a plan. No technical setup required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Within a couple of minutes you'll receive a welcome email with your login token and your AI's personal URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your token">
    Visit your AI's URL (e.g. `mark.madeformeai.com`) and paste the token from your email. Save it somewhere safe — it's your key to log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start talking">
    Your AI is ready immediately. No tutorial, no configuration. Just tell it what you need.
  </Step>
</Steps>
