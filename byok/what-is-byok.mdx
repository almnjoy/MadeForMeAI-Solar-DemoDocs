title: What is BYOK?
description: Bring your own API key and pay just for the platform.
---

BYOK stands for **Bring Your Own Key**.

If you already have an account with OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter and want to use your own API key, the BYOK plan lets you do that. You pay $15/month for the platform — and you pay your AI provider directly for the actual model usage.

## Who is BYOK for?

- People who already have an OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter account
- Developers or power users who want to control exactly which model they use
- Anyone who wants to keep AI costs separate and transparent

## How it works

1. You sign up for the BYOK plan ($15/mo)
2. Your instance is provisioned with a basic fallback model
3. You add your own API key in Settings
4. Your AI uses your key for all model calls — you're billed by your provider

## What does it cost in total?

- **Platform:** $15/month to MadeForMeAI
- **AI usage:** Billed directly by your provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter) based on your actual usage

For light-to-moderate use, total cost is typically well under $30/month combined — often cheaper than Standard.

## Not sure if BYOK is right for you?

If you don't already have an API key and don't want to deal with any setup, **Standard** is the easier choice. Everything is included and ready to go.
