The BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) plan is designed for people who already have an account with an AI provider — like OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter — and want to connect that account directly to their MadeForMeAI instance. Instead of paying a single bundled price, you pay $15/month for the MadeForMeAI platform and pay your AI provider separately, only for what you actually use.

## What BYOK means

BYOK stands for **Bring Your Own Key**. Rather than MadeForMeAI managing AI access on your behalf, you supply an API key from a supported provider. Every time your AI assistant makes a model call, it uses your key — and your provider bills you directly for that usage.

## Who is BYOK for?

BYOK is a strong fit if you:

- Already have an active account with OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter
- Want to choose exactly which AI model your assistant uses
- Prefer to keep your AI spending transparent and separated from your platform subscription
- Are a developer or power user comfortable managing API keys

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for the BYOK plan">
    Subscribe to BYOK at $15/month. Your MadeForMeAI instance is provisioned immediately with a basic fallback model so you can get started.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get an API key from your provider">
    Create an account (or log in) with OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter and generate an API key. See [Supported Providers](/byok/supported-providers) for details on each option.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your key in Settings">
    Open your AI instance, go to **Settings → API Keys**, and paste in your key. Your instance will begin using it immediately for all model calls.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick your model">
    Use the model picker in the chat interface to select a model from your provider. Your API key is used automatically for every conversation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Total cost breakdown

Your monthly spend has two components:

| Cost | Amount |
|---|---|
| MadeForMeAI platform | $15/month |
| AI model usage | Billed directly by your provider, based on actual use |

For light-to-moderate use, the combined total is typically well under $30/month — and often less than the Standard plan.

<Tip>
OpenRouter is the most cost-efficient starting point for most BYOK users. You get access to models from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, and more — all with a single key, and no monthly minimums.
</Tip>

## When to choose Standard instead

BYOK requires you to create an account with an external provider, manage an API key, and monitor your usage and credits. If you'd prefer a simpler experience where everything is included and ready the moment you sign up, **Standard** is the better choice.

<Note>
You can always start on Standard and switch to BYOK later if your needs change.
</Note>
