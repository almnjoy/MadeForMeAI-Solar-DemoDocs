title: How Memory Works
description: Why your AI remembers you, what it saves, and how to make the most of it.
---

Most AI tools forget everything the second you close the tab. Every session starts from zero. That's not how MadeForMeAI works.

Your AI has persistent memory. It builds up a picture of who you are over time, and uses that context every time you talk to it.

## What it remembers

Your AI saves things you tell it explicitly, and also learns patterns from your conversations over time. For example:

- Your name, what you do, where you live
- Your preferences ("I like bullet points, not walls of text")
- Your ongoing projects and goals
- People, places, and things you reference regularly
- How you communicate and what tone you prefer
- Deadlines and plans you've talked about

## How to teach it things

You can tell it things directly and it will save them:

- _"Remember that my business partner is named Kim."_
- _"I prefer short, direct answers — no fluff."_
- _"My biggest goal this year is to get 20 new clients by December."_

You can also just talk naturally. If you mention your kids, your business, your schedule — it picks up on those things and builds context automatically.

## What it doesn't automatically do

Memory isn't magic. Your AI won't know something unless you've told it or it came up in a conversation. A few things to keep in mind:

- **It doesn't read your email or calendar unless you connect them.** [Connecting apps →](/connect/overview)
- **It doesn't know what's on your computer.** Files on your desktop don't exist for your AI unless you upload them. [Why →](/understanding/cloud-not-local)
- **If you reset your AI, memory is wiped.** This is an option in your account dashboard, and it's permanent. Don't use it unless you actually want a fresh start.

## Updating something it remembered wrong

Just correct it naturally:

_"Actually, my business is in Dallas now, not Austin — update that."_

_"I changed my mind on that — I'd rather get longer, detailed answers now."_

Your AI will update what it knows.

## Memory across different chats

Your AI has one memory that persists across all your conversations. It doesn't matter if you start a new conversation — it's the same assistant with the same history.

If you're using your AI through WhatsApp or Telegram in addition to the main chat, memory is still shared — it all goes to the same assistant.

---

The short version: **tell it things once, and it will remember them forever.** The more context you give it, the more useful it becomes over time.
