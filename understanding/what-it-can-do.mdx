Here's a straight list of what your AI can do right now, what needs a little setup first, and what it genuinely can't do yet.

## Works right now, no setup needed

These all work the moment you log in:

**Writing and editing**
- Draft emails, messages, proposals, social posts, bios, cover letters
- Edit and improve writing you paste in — tone, length, clarity
- Translate to other languages
- Write in a specific voice or style

**Research and information**
- Search the web and summarize what it finds
- Answer questions on any topic
- Compare options and give you a recommendation
- Summarize long documents you upload

**Planning and thinking**
- Build a week-by-week plan for a project or goal
- Help you prioritize when you have too much going on
- Think through a decision with you
- Brainstorm ideas, names, angles, strategies

**Everyday tasks**
- Draft a response to a difficult email or message
- Write a review, complaint, or feedback message
- Help you figure out what to say in a tricky situation
- Put together a checklist, template, or script

**Files you upload**
- Read and summarize PDFs, Word docs, spreadsheets
- Pull out key information from documents
- Answer questions about a specific file

## Works with a quick setup

These are powerful but need you to connect something first:

**WhatsApp or Telegram** — Talk to your AI on your phone through an app you already use. You text it, it texts back. [Connect WhatsApp →](/connect/whatsapp) | [Connect Telegram →](/connect/telegram)

**Gmail** — Read, draft, and send emails through your AI. Tell it to _"check my inbox and summarize anything important"_ or _"reply to the email from Kim about the invoice."_ [Connect Gmail →](/connect/gmail-google)

**Google Drive** — Access, read, create, and organize files in your Drive. [Connect Google Drive →](/connect/google-drive)

## Things people try that don't work (yet)

**Access files on your computer directly** — Your AI runs in the cloud, not on your laptop. It can't see your desktop, your Downloads folder, or any local file. You have to upload files into the chat for it to see them. [Why →](/understanding/cloud-not-local)

**Make phone calls or send texts** — Not available by default. (Voice calling via ElevenLabs is possible with advanced setup — ask in Discord.)

**Remember things across a total reset** — If you use the "Reset Agent" option in your dashboard, all memory is permanently wiped. That's a manual action you'd have to take on purpose.

## Real things people do with it every day

These are actual use cases from real users:

- _"What was that idea I had last week about [topic]?"_ — It knows, because you told it.
- _"Summarize my last 10 emails and tell me what needs a response."_ — Works once Gmail is connected.
- _"I have a job interview tomorrow at [company]. Help me prepare."_ — Web research plus coaching in one shot.
- _"Write a Yelp response to this negative review."_ — Paste the review, get a draft in seconds.
- _"Help me build a simple content calendar for my Instagram for the next month."_ — Done in 2 minutes.
- _"I have $500 to spend on ads. Walk me through how to use it."_ — Strategy and step-by-step guidance.

---

The best way to find out what it can do is to just ask. Start with _"what can you help me with?"_ and go from there.
