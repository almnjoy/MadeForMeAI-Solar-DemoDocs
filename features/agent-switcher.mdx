Your AI isn't just one thing. Depending on what you've set up, you may have multiple agents available — each one focused on a different job. The agent switcher lets you move between them without starting a new session or losing any context.

---

## Where to find it

Look at the **top-left corner of your chat window**. You'll see the name of the agent you're currently talking to. Click or tap it to open the switcher and see everything available.

---

## What agents you might have

| Agent | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Main Assistant** | Your primary AI — general tasks, research, writing, planning |
| **Setup Assistant** | Guides you through connecting apps and configuring features |
| **Job Search Agent** | Monitors job boards, scores postings, notifies you of strong matches — [learn more](/features/job-search-agent) |

You may have more or fewer depending on what you've activated. Each agent has its own workspace, memory, and purpose.

---

## How switching works

Switching agents doesn't end your conversation with the current one — it just moves you to a different one. When you switch back, everything is where you left it.

Each agent has its own:
- Conversation history
- Workspace files
- Configuration and preferences

---

## Adding new agents

New agents are activated through the **Setup Assistant**. Just tell your main AI what you want:

- _"Set up a job search agent"_
- _"I want a new agent for [task]"_

The Setup Assistant handles the provisioning. Once done, the new agent appears in the switcher automatically.

---

## Don't see the switcher?

If you only have one agent, the switcher may not be visible — there's nothing to switch to. Activate a second agent (like the Job Search Agent) and it'll appear.
