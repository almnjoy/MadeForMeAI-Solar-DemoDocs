Your AI can search the web and bring back current information — news, prices, research, anything.

## How to use it

Just ask. If your question needs up-to-date information, your AI will search automatically.

- *"What's the weather in Chicago this weekend?"*
- *"Find me the latest news about [topic]."*
- *"What's the current price of [product]?"*
- *"Research competitors for a meal prep delivery service."*

You don't need to say "search the web" — it figures out when to search on its own.

## What it's good for

- Current events and news
- Product research and comparisons
- Finding specific information quickly
- Pulling together research on a topic

## Limitations

- It searches the public web — it can't access pages behind logins
- Results are only as good as what's publicly available
- For very recent events (last few hours), results may be limited
