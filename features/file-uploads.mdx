---
title: File Uploads
description: Send documents, images, and files to your AI for analysis.
---

You can upload files directly to your AI and ask it to work with them.

## What you can upload

- **Documents:** PDF, Word (.docx), text files
- **Spreadsheets:** CSV, Excel
- **Images:** PNG, JPG, screenshots
- **Code files:** Any plain text file

## How to upload

Click the attachment icon in the chat input, select your file, and send it along with your message.

## What to ask it to do

Once you upload a file, just tell it what you need:

- *"Summarize this contract in plain English."*
- *"What are the key numbers in this spreadsheet?"*
- *"Find any errors in this document."*
- *"Rewrite this in a friendlier tone."*
- *"What's in this screenshot?"*

## Tips

- You can upload multiple files in one message
- Larger files take a few seconds to process — just wait
- If you upload a long document, be specific about what you want: *"Focus on section 3"* works better than *"read this"*
