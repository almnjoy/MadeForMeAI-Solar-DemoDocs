Your MadeForMeAI setup comes with two agents out of the box:

## Main Agent

This is the one you talk to every day. It handles:

- General conversation and Q&A
- Writing, editing, summarizing
- Research and web search
- Planning, brainstorming, decision support
- File and document work
- Anything connected through your apps (Gmail, Google Drive, WhatsApp, etc.)

This is your primary assistant. When you go to `yourname.madeformeai.com` and start typing, you're talking to this agent.

## Setup Assistant

A dedicated agent for connecting apps and configuring your setup. It follows a structured process, speaks in plain English, and is specifically designed to work with non-technical users.

You activate it by asking for app setup: _"Set up WhatsApp"_, _"Connect my Gmail"_, etc.
