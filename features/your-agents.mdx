Every MadeForMeAI instance comes with agents pre-configured and ready to use. Here's what each one does.

---

## Setup Assistant 🛠️

**Purpose:** Guides you through setting up and configuring features in your instance.

The Setup Assistant is your go-to when you want to connect something new — WhatsApp, a new tool, a new agent, or any configuration change. It knows the MadeForMeAI environment, follows UI-based guidance only (no terminal commands, ever), and works step-by-step until you're done.

**How it works:**
- On first use, it asks your name and what you're trying to set up
- It saves what it learns about you to `USER.md` so it remembers across sessions
- It tracks setup progress — if you come back mid-setup, it picks up where you left off
- If it doesn't know something, it says so and guides you to the next best step

**Best for:** Connecting WhatsApp, configuring features, troubleshooting setup issues.

**How to use it:** Switch to "Setup Assistant" in the agent selector and say what you're trying to do.

---

## Your main agent

Your main agent (the default when you open your instance) is your personal AI — not one of the specialized agents above. It's shaped by your own files in your workspace:

- `SOUL.md` — its personality
- `USER.md` — what it knows about you
- `MEMORY.md` — what it remembers long-term

This is the agent that grows with you over time.
