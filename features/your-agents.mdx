Every MadeForMeAI instance comes with agents pre-configured and ready to use. Here's what each one does.

---

## Setup Assistant 🛠️

**Purpose:** Guides you through setting up and configuring features in your instance.

The Setup Assistant is your go-to when you want to connect something new — WhatsApp, a new tool, a new agent, or any configuration change. It knows the MadeForMeAI environment, follows UI-based guidance only (no terminal commands, ever), and works step-by-step until you're done.

**How it works:**
- On first use, it asks your name and what you're trying to set up
- It saves what it learns about you to `USER.md` so it remembers across sessions
- It tracks setup progress — if you come back mid-setup, it picks up where you left off
- If it doesn't know something, it says so and guides you to the next best step

**Best for:** Connecting WhatsApp, configuring features, troubleshooting setup issues.

**How to use it:** Switch to "Setup Assistant" in the agent selector and say what you're trying to do.

---

## Founder 🚀

**Purpose:** Answers questions about MadeForMeAI the way the founder would — directly, simply, and without the corporate tone.

This agent represents Dustin (the person who built this). It knows the product, understands the thinking behind it, and can explain anything from "what even is this" to "what should I do next" in plain language.

It doesn't overwhelm. It doesn't pad responses. It guides.

**How it responds:**
- Conversational, not structured
- Recommends one thing instead of listing five options
- Tells you the next step rather than explaining everything at once

**Best for:** Understanding what MadeForMeAI is, what it can do, and where to start.

**How to use it:** Switch to "Founder" and ask whatever's on your mind.

---

## Discord Support 🛠️

**Purpose:** Support-focused help, tuned for troubleshooting and step-by-step guidance.

Similar to the Founder agent but in support mode — calmer, more methodical, focused on getting you unblocked. It references the MadeForMeAI docs, translates documentation into plain steps, and doesn't guess when it doesn't know.

**How it responds:**
- Step-by-step when walking through a fix
- Asks for specifics (what you see, what step you're on, any errors) before diagnosing
- References docs but explains them in plain language

**Best for:** Something isn't working, you're stuck mid-setup, or you need a clear fix.

**How to use it:** Switch to "Discord Support" and describe what's happening.

---

## Your main agent

Your main agent (the default when you open your instance) is your personal AI — not one of the specialized agents above. It's shaped by your own files in your workspace:

- `SOUL.md` — its personality
- `USER.md` — what it knows about you
- `MEMORY.md` — what it remembers long-term

This is the agent that grows with you over time.
