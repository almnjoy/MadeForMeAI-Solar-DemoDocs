Memory is what makes MadeForMeAI different from every other AI tool.

## How it works

Everything you tell your AI gets stored in its memory. Next time you open a conversation, it already knows everything from before.

You never start from scratch. No copy-pasting context. No re-explaining who you are.

## What it remembers

- Facts you've told it (your job, your goals, your projects)
- Your preferences (how you like things done, your tone)
- Ongoing work (projects you're in the middle of, decisions you've made)
- People and places you've mentioned

## Examples

**You on Monday:**
> "I'm trying to launch my online store by June 1st. I'm behind on the product photos."

**You on Thursday:**
> "How am I doing on that June deadline?"

Your AI already knows what you're talking about.

---

**You once:**
> "I hate long emails. Keep everything short and direct."

From then on, every email draft it writes for you will be short and direct — without you asking.

## Telling it to remember something specific

Just say it naturally:

> *"Remember that my client Sarah prefers to be contacted on Tuesdays."*
> *"Keep in mind that I'm vegetarian when helping me plan meals."*

## Telling it to forget something

> *"Forget what I told you about the Johnson project — that's been cancelled."*

## Is my memory private?

Yes. Your memory is stored only in your private instance. Nobody else can read it, access it, or use it to train any AI model.
