Your AI has persistent memory. That means when you come back tomorrow, next week, or next month — it still knows who you are, what you care about, and what you've talked about before.

This is one of the biggest things that separates MadeForMeAI from tools like ChatGPT, which forgets everything when you close the tab.

## What gets remembered

Your AI builds up a picture of you over time by:

- **Things you tell it directly** — "I prefer bullet points," "My business partner is named Kim," "I'm trying to lose weight this year"
- **Things that come up naturally** — your job, your goals, your habits, the people you mention
- **How you communicate** — whether you like short answers or detailed ones, formal or casual tone

## How to use it intentionally

The fastest way to make your AI more useful is to tell it things upfront:

_"Remember these things about me: I run a dog grooming business in Phoenix, I have 3 employees, and my busiest time is Saturday mornings. I'm trying to grow from 40 to 60 clients this year."_

It saves all of that. Every future conversation starts from that foundation.

## Correcting something it remembered wrong

Just say so:

_"Actually I moved to Denver — update that."_
_"I changed my mind on that. I prefer longer answers now."_

It adjusts.

## Memory across apps

If you use your AI through WhatsApp, Telegram, or the browser — it's the same assistant with the same memory. It doesn't matter how you reach it.

## Resetting memory

You can wipe all memory from your account dashboard using the **Reset Agent** option. This permanently deletes everything — conversations, memory, and files. Your subscription stays active.

Only do this if you genuinely want a fresh start. It cannot be undone.
