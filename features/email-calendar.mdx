---
title: "Connect Gmail and Google Calendar to Your MadeForMeAI"
description: "Link your Google account to MadeForMeAI so your AI can read emails, draft replies, check your calendar, and help you stay on top of your day."
---

When you connect your Google account, your AI gains access to your Gmail inbox and Google Calendar — and can start doing real work with them. Ask it to summarize your unread emails, draft a reply, check your schedule for conflicts, or book a meeting. Your AI handles the back-and-forth so you don't have to.

## What you can do

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Gmail">
    With Gmail connected, you can ask your AI things like:

    - *"What emails need my attention today?"*
    - *"Draft a reply to Sarah about the project update."*
    - *"Find the invoice email from last month."*
    - *"Summarize what's going on with the Johnson account."*
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google Calendar">
    With Google Calendar connected, you can ask your AI things like:

    - *"What does my week look like?"*
    - *"Am I free Thursday at 2pm?"*
    - *"Schedule a 30-minute call with Mike next week."*
    - *"What's my next meeting?"*
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to connect your Google account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings or Plugins">
    In your AI's chat interface, navigate to the **Settings** or **Plugins** area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Google integration">
    Look for the Google / Gmail / Calendar integration option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click **Connect** and complete authorization through Google's login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're all set">
    Once authorized, your AI can immediately see your inbox and calendar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can revoke access at any time. Go to [myaccount.google.com](https://myaccount.google.com) → **Security** → **Third-party apps** and remove MadeForMeAI from the list.
</Note>

## Privacy

Your AI reads your emails and calendar events on your behalf — but that data stays entirely within your private instance. It is not shared with anyone, not used to train any AI model, and not stored outside your account.
