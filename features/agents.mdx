title: What Are Agents?
description: Your AI has different modes for different jobs — here's what that means.
---

Your AI isn't just one static thing. It can operate in different **modes** (called agents) depending on what you need it to do.

Think of it like having one assistant who can switch hats depending on the job — the same person, but operating in a focused mode for the task at hand.

## The agents you have by default

**Your main AI** is what you interact with every day — it handles conversations, answers questions, writes things, manages your tasks, and connects to your apps.

**The Setup Assistant** is a specialized mode that helps you connect apps and configure features. It's designed to work well with non-technical users and give step-by-step guidance without overwhelming you. [Learn more →](/features/setup-assistant)

## Why this matters

Specialized agents can focus on one job without getting confused by everything else. Your Setup Assistant, for example, follows a strict process and doesn't get distracted by off-topic questions — it's built to handle one thing and do it reliably.

Over time, MadeForMeAI may offer more specialized agents for specific use cases. For now, your main AI handles the vast majority of what you'll need day to day.

## You don't need to think about this much

Unless you're specifically doing app setup, you're always talking to your main AI. The agent stuff happens in the background. You just talk.
