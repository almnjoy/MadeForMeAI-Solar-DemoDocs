MadeForMeAI runs on a large language model under the hood, and you can change which model it uses without leaving the chat. Different models have different strengths — some are faster, others are more capable for complex tasks. The right choice depends on what you're working on and which plan you're on.

## How to switch models

Look for the model selector dropdown at the top of the chat interface. Click it to see the models available on your plan, then select the one you want to use.

## Which model should you use?

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Standard and Power plans">
    Your AI comes pre-configured with **Claude Sonnet** — a strong, well-rounded model that handles most tasks well. The majority of users never need to change it.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="BYOK plan">
    Your AI defaults to a fast, budget-friendly model. Once you've [added your API key](/byok/adding-your-key), you can switch to a more powerful model from the same dropdown.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Tips for picking the right model

- **Faster models** work well for quick tasks: drafting, answering questions, light editing.
- **More capable models** are better suited for complex analysis, long documents, and writing that requires nuance.
- **If a model isn't responding**, try switching to a different one — it's the fastest way to get back on track.

<Tip>
  If you're not sure which model to use, stick with the default. It's pre-selected for a reason and handles the vast majority of everyday tasks without any adjustments needed.
</Tip>
