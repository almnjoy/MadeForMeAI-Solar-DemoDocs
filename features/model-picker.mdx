Your AI runs on a large language model under the hood. You can switch which model it uses at any time directly from the chat header — no settings menu, no digging around.

## How to switch

Look for the model name displayed at the top of your chat window. Click it to open the model picker and choose a different one.

---

## Which one should I use?

The default — **Smart Assistant** — handles most things well. You only need to switch when you have a specific type of task.

| Option | Best for |
|---|---|
| ⭐ **Smart Assistant** | Everyday tasks, research, writing, planning — your default |
| ⚡ **Quick Answers** | Fast, simple questions where speed matters more than depth |
| 🧠 **Logic & Reasoning** | Complex decisions, step-by-step analysis, math, structured thinking |
| 💻 **Coding Helper** | Writing, reviewing, or debugging code |
| 🔬 **Deep Research** | Long documents, thorough analysis — slower but more thorough |
| 💎 **Premium Claude** | Anthropic's Claude Sonnet — when you want Anthropic's best |
| 💸 **Ultra Cheap** | High-volume repetitive tasks where cost matters |

---

## Tips

- **Start with Smart Assistant.** It's good at almost everything.
- **Switch to Deep Research** when you've pasted in a long document or need a careful, thorough answer.
- **Switch to Quick Answers** when you're just asking simple questions and want faster responses.
- **If a model stops responding**, switch to a different one — sometimes a specific model has a brief outage on the provider's end.
- Your model choice **doesn't carry over** between conversations — it resets to the default each session unless you change your default in settings.
