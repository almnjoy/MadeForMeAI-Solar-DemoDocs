Setting up new apps and connectors sounds technical. It doesn't have to be.

Every MadeForMeAI instance includes a **Setup Assistant** — a dedicated AI mode built specifically to guide you through connecting apps and configuring features. It reads the setup guide for whatever you're trying to do before it says a word, so it knows exactly what steps to walk you through.

## How to use it

You don't have to switch modes or find a special button. Just ask your AI in plain English:

- _"Set up WhatsApp for me"_
- _"Connect Telegram"_
- _"I want to use Discord"_
- _"Set up a job search agent"_

Your AI will hand off to the Setup Assistant automatically, which will walk you through each step one at a time.

## What the Setup Assistant does

- Reads the correct setup guide for your request before starting
- Asks you clarifying questions if it needs to
- Gives you clear, numbered steps — one at a time, no walls of text
- Waits for your confirmation before doing anything
- Handles all the behind-the-scenes configuration
- Tells you when it's done and how to verify it worked

You never need to open a config file, run a command, or do anything technical. The Setup Assistant handles all of that.

## If something goes wrong

Just tell it. _"That didn't work"_ or _"I didn't see the QR code"_ or _"I got an error that says [paste it here]."_

The Setup Assistant knows how to troubleshoot common issues. If something is genuinely broken on our end, it'll tell you and point you to [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) where you can get direct help.
