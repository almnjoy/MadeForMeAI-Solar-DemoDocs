Setting up new apps and connectors sounds technical. It doesn't have to be.

Every MadeForMeAI instance includes a **Setup Assistant** — a second AI mode specifically built to guide non-technical users through connecting apps and configuring features. You just tell it what you want, and it handles the steps.

## How to use it

You don't have to switch modes or find a special button. Just ask your AI in plain English:

- _"Set up WhatsApp for me"_
- _"Connect my Gmail"_
- _"I want to use Telegram"_
- _"Set up Google Drive"_

Your AI will hand off to the Setup Assistant automatically, which will walk you through each step one at a time.

## What the Setup Assistant does

- Asks you clarifying questions if it needs to
- Gives you clear, numbered steps — one at a time, no walls of text
- Waits for your confirmation before doing anything
- Handles all the behind-the-scenes configuration for you
- Tells you when it's done and how to verify it worked

You never need to open a config file, run a command, or do anything technical. The Setup Assistant handles all of that.

## If something goes wrong

Just tell it. _"That didn't work"_ or _"I didn't see the QR code"_ or _"I got an error that says [copy the error here]."_

The Setup Assistant knows how to troubleshoot common issues and will walk you through a fix.

If it's something it genuinely can't resolve, it'll tell you to reach out in the [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) where Dustin and the community can help directly.

## What it can set up

Currently supported through the Setup Assistant:

- WhatsApp
- Discord
- Telegram
- Gmail and Google Calendar
- Google Drive
- ElevenLabs voice (send and receive audio messages)

More are added regularly. If you want something not on this list, ask in the Discord.
