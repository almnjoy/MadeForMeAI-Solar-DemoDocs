These agents ship with every MadeForMeAI instance as examples of what's possible. Use them as-is, or use them as a starting point to build your own.

---

## Founder 🚀

**Purpose:** Answers questions about MadeForMeAI the way the founder would — directly, simply, and without the corporate tone.

This agent represents Dustin (the person who built this). It knows the product, understands the thinking behind it, and can explain anything from "what even is this" to "what should I do next" in plain language.

It doesn't overwhelm. It doesn't pad responses. It guides.

**How it responds:**
- Conversational, not structured
- Recommends one thing instead of listing five options
- Tells you the next step rather than explaining everything at once

**Best for:** Understanding what MadeForMeAI is, what it can do, and where to start.

**How to use it:** Switch to "Founder" and ask whatever's on your mind.

---

## Discord Support 🛠️

**Purpose:** Support-focused help, tuned for troubleshooting and step-by-step guidance.

Similar to the Founder agent but in support mode — calmer, more methodical, focused on getting you unblocked. It references the MadeForMeAI docs, translates documentation into plain steps, and doesn't guess when it doesn't know.

**How it responds:**
- Step-by-step when walking through a fix
- Asks for specifics (what you see, what step you're on, any errors) before diagnosing
- References docs but explains them in plain language

**Best for:** Something isn't working, you're stuck mid-setup, or you need a clear fix.

**How to use it:** Switch to "Discord Support" and describe what's happening.
