Not sure what to do with your AI? Here are real things people use it for every day.

## Small business owners

- Writing client proposals, quotes, and follow-up emails
- Building a simple content calendar for social media
- Drafting responses to reviews (positive and negative)
- Thinking through pricing, packaging, or service offerings
- Summarizing competitor research from the web
- Writing job postings or onboarding documents for new employees

## Everyday life

- Helping organize a busy week when things pile up
- Writing difficult messages (to a landlord, a boss, a family member)
- Researching a major purchase and comparing options
- Planning a trip — itinerary, budget, packing list
- Summarizing a long document or contract in plain English
- Getting a second opinion on a decision

## Learning and productivity

- Explaining something confusing in simple terms
- Helping study or understand a complex topic
- Turning raw notes into a clean summary or action plan
- Brainstorming names, ideas, or approaches for a project

## Connected to apps (after setup)

- _"Check my Gmail and tell me what needs a response today"_
- _"Find the proposal in my Google Drive and update the pricing section"_
- _"Send my business partner a WhatsApp message that I'll be 10 minutes late"_
- _"Add a reminder to my Google Calendar for next Tuesday at 2pm"_
