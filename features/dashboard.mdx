Your dashboard at [madeformeai.com/dashboard](https://madeformeai.com/dashboard) is the home base for your AI. From here you can access your AI, manage your account, view your files, and handle anything administrative without ever needing to contact support.

<img src="/images/dashboard-user.png" alt="User Dashboard" />

---

## Account Info

Your dashboard shows your current plan, the email on your account, and your personal AI URL — the unique address where your AI lives. If you ever forget your AI's address, this is where to find it.

---

## Logging In to Your AI

There are two ways to open your AI directly from the dashboard.

**SSO Login** signs you in automatically using your MadeForMeAI account — no second password needed. Click **Launch AI** and you're in.

<img src="/images/sso-login.png" alt="SSO Login" />

**Web Chat** opens your AI directly in the browser at your personal URL. Use this if you want to share the link with yourself, bookmark it, or open it on a different device.

---

## File System

Every account includes a personal file space at `{username}.files.madeformeai.com`. This is a live, persistent file browser directly linked to your AI's workspace.

<img src="/images/files-user.png" alt="File System" />

You can:
- **Upload files** so your AI can read and work with them
- **Download files** your AI has created or modified
- **Browse your workspace** the same way your AI sees it

Files you upload here are immediately available to your AI in conversation. Files your AI saves appear here automatically — no manual syncing needed.

---

## Reset Pod

The **Reset Pod** option does a hard restart of your AI container. Use this if your AI becomes unresponsive, slow, or behaves unexpectedly. It's the equivalent of rebooting — your memory, files, and settings are all preserved. It typically takes 30–60 seconds to come back online.

This is almost always the fix for a stuck or laggy AI before filing a bug report.

---

## Send Bug Report

If something isn't working right, the **Bug Report** button sends your report directly to us with your account details automatically attached. You don't need to include your username or plan — just describe what happened and when.

For fastest resolution, include:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Whether a reset pod fixed it or not
