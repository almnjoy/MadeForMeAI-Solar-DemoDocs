Cancelling your MadeForMeAI plan is straightforward — no forms, no calls, no runaround. You can do it yourself directly from your billing portal, and your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

## How to cancel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your customer portal">
    Use the link in your welcome email to access the portal. If you can't find it, email support@madeformeai.com and we'll resend it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel your subscription">
    Select the cancellation option in the portal. It takes one click to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period. You won't lose access immediately after cancelling.
</Note>

## What happens to your data

After you cancel, here's what to expect:

1. **Your instance stays up** until the end of your billing period
2. **At the end of the period**, your AI goes offline and your URL stops working
3. **Your data is held for 30 days** in case you decide to come back
4. **After 30 days**, all your data is permanently deleted — conversations, files, memory, everything

<Warning>
  After 30 days, your data cannot be recovered. If you think you might return, make sure to resubscribe within that window so we can restore it for you.
</Warning>

## Export your data before you go

Before your access ends, you can download your conversations and files directly from within your AI.

<Info>
  A formal export tool is in the works. For now, copy or save anything important before your billing period ends.
</Info>

## Come back after cancelling

If you resubscribe within 30 days of cancellation, contact us and we'll restore your data.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Resubscribe">
    Sign up for a new plan at MadeForMeAI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact us">
    Email support@madeformeai.com or reach out on [Discord](https://discord.gg/madeformeai) and let us know you've resubscribed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We'll restore your data">
    We'll reconnect your previous conversations, files, and memory to your new instance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Refunds

We don't offer refunds for partial months. If something went wrong on our end, reach out — email support@madeformeai.com or ping us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/madeformeai) and we'll make it right.
