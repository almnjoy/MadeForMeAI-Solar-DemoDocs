MadeForMeAI has three plans. Here's what they mean in plain English.

## Plans

| Plan | Monthly | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **BYOK** | $15/mo | You already pay for OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter separately |
| **Standard** | $30/mo | Most people — everything included, ready to go |
| **Power** | $60/mo | Heavy daily users who need faster responses and higher limits |

Your first 30 days are included after activation. Cancel anytime.

---

## Standard — the right choice for most people

Standard includes everything you need. The AI is powered and ready to go — no extra accounts, no extra setup, no surprise costs.

You get generous daily usage limits that cover normal use: writing, research, planning, chatting, working with files, and connecting apps. Most people never hit the limit.

---

## Power — for heavy daily users

Power gives you higher usage limits and access to faster, more capable models. If you're using your AI multiple hours a day, hitting limits, or doing complex work that needs more processing power — Power is for you.

---

## BYOK — if you already have an API key

BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) is for people who already pay for API access from OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter. You bring your own key, we run the private infrastructure, and you pay just $15/month for the platform.

With BYOK, your AI usage costs come from your provider directly. Usage is billed by your API provider based on how much you use.

[Setting up BYOK →](/byok/setup)

---

## Upgrading or downgrading

To change your plan, email billing-support@madeformeai.com. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

## What happens after 30 days

After your free activation period, your card is charged monthly on the same date you activated. You'll get an email receipt each time.
