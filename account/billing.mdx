## How billing works

You're billed monthly, starting 30 days after you activate your account. Your card is charged automatically each month on the same date.

You'll get an email receipt after each charge. If a payment fails, you'll get an email with instructions to update your card.

For billing questions or to change your payment method: **billing-support@madeformeai.com**

## Cancellation

To cancel, email **billing-support@madeformeai.com** from the address you signed up with. No forms, no retention flow, no runaround. Just ask and it's done.

## What happens to your data after cancellation

Your data — conversations, memory, files, connected apps — is held for **30 days** after cancellation. If you resubscribe within that window, everything can be restored exactly as you left it.

After 30 days, all data is **permanently deleted**. We cannot recover it after that point.

## Refunds

We don't offer prorated refunds for unused time within a billing period. If you cancel mid-cycle, your access continues until the end of that period.

If you had a billing issue (double charge, charge after cancellation, etc.), email billing-support@madeformeai.com and we'll make it right.
