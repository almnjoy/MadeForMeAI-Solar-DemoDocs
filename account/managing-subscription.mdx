Your MadeForMeAI subscription is managed through LemonSqueezy, our payment processor. Everything billing-related — from viewing invoices to updating your card — lives in your personal customer portal, accessible via a link in your welcome email.

## Access your billing portal

Your welcome email contains a direct link to your customer portal. Open it to manage all aspects of your subscription in one place.

From the portal you can:

- View your current plan and next billing date
- Update your payment method
- Download past invoices
- Cancel your subscription

<Tip>
  Can't find your welcome email? Reach out to us at support@madeformeai.com and we'll resend the portal link.
</Tip>

## Upgrade your plan

Self-serve plan upgrades are coming soon. In the meantime, contact us to move between plans — for example, from Standard to Power, or from BYOK to Standard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your preferred channel">
    Reach out on [Discord](https://discord.gg/madeformeai) or email support@madeformeai.com.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tell us which plan you want">
    Let us know your current plan and the plan you'd like to move to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We'll handle the rest">
    We'll process the upgrade manually and confirm once it's done.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change your payment method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your customer portal">
    Use the link in your welcome email to access the portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Update payment method">
    Enter your new card details and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Billing date

You're billed on the same calendar day each month as your original signup date. For example, if you subscribed on the 12th, your renewal runs on the 12th of every month.

## Failed payments

If a payment fails, LemonSqueezy retries it automatically. You'll receive an email notification if there's a problem with your payment.

<Warning>
  If your subscription becomes past-due, your instance will remain active for a short grace period. Update your payment method in the customer portal before the grace period ends to avoid any interruption to your service.
</Warning>
