title: Managing Your Subscription
description: Update billing, change payment method, or view your plan.
---

Your subscription is managed through Stripe, our payment processor.

## Viewing your account

Log in to your MadeForMeAI instance to see your current plan. For billing details, invoices, or payment method changes, email billing-support@madeformeai.com and we'll help you out.

- View your current plan and billing date
- Update your payment method
- Download invoices
- Cancel your subscription

If you need help with any of these, email billing-support@madeformeai.com.

## Upgrading your plan

Want to move from Standard to Power, or BYOK to Standard? Reach out in [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) or email support@madeformeai.com — we'll handle it manually while self-serve upgrades are being built.

## Changing your payment method

Email billing-support@madeformeai.com and we'll send you a secure link to update your payment method.

## Billing date

You're billed on the same day each month as your original signup date.

## Failed payments

If a payment fails, Stripe will retry automatically. You'll get an email if there's an issue. If your subscription goes past-due your instance will remain active for a short grace period — update your payment method before it lapses.
